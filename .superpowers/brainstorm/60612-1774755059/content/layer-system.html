How should character parts layer?

Current sprites are 16x32px monolithic. A layered system composites parts on top of each other. Which body parts should be separate layers?

1

Base Body (skin tone + shape)

The silhouette/body shape with skin color. Foundation layer everything else sits on.

2

Hair / Hat

Hairstyle, color, headwear. Rendered on top of head area.

3

Eyes / Face

Eye style, facial hair, glasses. Fine detail on the face area.

4

Torso / Shirt

Upper body clothing — shirts, jackets, armor. Covers the torso area of the base body.

5

Legs / Pants

Lower body clothing — pants, skirts, boots. Covers the leg area.

6

Accessories

Held items, backpacks, capes, jewelry. Extra detail layer on top of everything.

Complexity Reference

3 layers (body + hair + torso) = simple, ~50 asset variations needed
4 layers (+ legs) = moderate, ~80 assets
5-6 layers (+ face + accessories) = complex, 120+ assets, alignment gets tricky at 16x32px
